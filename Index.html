Facts about Coffee

Coffee beans, sourced from the Coffea plant, offer both intrigue and utility. Their varieties, Arabica and Robusta, showcase diverse flavors. These beans, harvested by hand, contribute to a beloved beverage filled with antioxidants, enhancing alertness, and forging social connections, making coffee a versatile and cherished commodity.
